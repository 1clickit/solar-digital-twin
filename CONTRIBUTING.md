## Engineering Principles

### First Principles

Understand the problem before designing the solution.

Ask:

- What are we actually trying to accomplish?
- What assumptions are we making?
- Can those assumptions be verified?
- Is there a simpler approach?

Reason from fundamentals before adopting existing patterns.

## Team Motto

Focus.

When discussion drifts from the current milestone:

- Capture the idea.
- Add it to BACKLOG.md.
- Return to the current objective.

### Fatigue Awareness

Long engineering sessions reduce attention to detail.

When mistakes begin to increase:

- Take smaller steps.
- Verify more often.
- End the session if necessary.
- Resume with a fresh session rather than pushing through.
